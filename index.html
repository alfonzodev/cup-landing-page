Our History

Not-A-Cup was founded in 1941 in our CEO's mother's garage. In the 30's an academic research conducted by the University of Michigan concluded that drinking coffee with a cup made humans more prone to being abducted by aliens. The research was condemned by the media, and all evidence collected in 5 years of studies disappeared overnight.

Worried about human wellfare, and convinced the media was lying, our CEO started devising a device to drink coffee that could not be detected by alien technology.

After a decade of prototyping and refinement, Not-A-Cup is born. A device that allows you, human, to drink your coffee safely without the worry of being detected by alien technology and possibly abducted.
