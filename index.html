Our History

Not-A-Cup was founded in 1941 in our CEO's mother's garage. In the 30's there was an academic research that concluded that drinking coffee with a cup made humans more prone to being abducted by aliens. The research was condemned by the media and all documentation and evidence collected in the research disappeared.

Worried about human wellfare, and not convinced by the lies the media told him, our CEO started devising a device to drink coffee that could not be detected by alien technology.

After a decade of prototyping and refinement, Not-A-Cup is born. A device that allows you, human, to drink your coffee safely without the worry of being detected by alien technology and possibly kidnapped.
